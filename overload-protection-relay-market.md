<p><strong>The Overload Protection Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Overload</li><li>Magnetic Overload</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overload protection relay market is primarily segmented into thermal and magnetic overload relays. Thermal overload relays operate by detecting excess current through heating elements, providing protection against overheating in motors. They are often used in applications with consistent loads. On the other hand, magnetic overload relays use electromagnetic principles to respond to sudden surges in current, offering rapid protection primarily in high-inrush situations. Both types are essential for ensuring the safety and longevity of electrical equipment in various industrial applications.</p></p>
