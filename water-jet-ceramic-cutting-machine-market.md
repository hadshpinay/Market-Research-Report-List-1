<p><strong>The Water-jet Ceramic Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasive Water-jet</li><li>Pure Water-jet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water-jet Ceramic Cutting Machine market comprises two primary types: Abrasive Water-jet and Pure Water-jet systems. Abrasive Water-jet machines incorporate an abrasive material mixed with water, enabling them to cut tough ceramics and other hard materials with precision. In contrast, Pure Water-jet machines utilize only high-pressure water streams, making them ideal for softer materials or applications requiring minimal kerf. Both types are valued for their versatility, accuracy, and ability to create intricate designs without thermal damage to the materials.</p></p>
