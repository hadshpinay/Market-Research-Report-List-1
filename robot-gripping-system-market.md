<p><p>The Robot Gripping System market encompasses various types of grippers designed for diverse automation tasks. Electric grippers utilize motors for precise control and adaptability, making them ideal for handling delicate or heavy objects. Pneumatic grippers rely on compressed air, offering high speed and strength, suitable for high-volume applications. Vacuum grippers use suction to pick and place items, effective for handling flat or smooth surfaces. Magnetic grippers utilize magnetic force to secure ferrous materials, providing a reliable solution for industrial applications.</p></p>
