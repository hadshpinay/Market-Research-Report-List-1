<p><p>The environmentally degradable refuse sacks market is categorized into two main types: biodegradable and photodegradable materials. Biodegradable materials, such as starch-based polymers, decompose through microbial action, breaking down into natural substances over time. In contrast, photodegradable materials contain additives that enable them to degrade when exposed to ultraviolet light, typically breaking down faster in outdoor conditions. Both types aim to reduce environmental impact by minimizing plastic waste and offering sustainable alternatives for disposal, enhancing eco-friendliness in waste management.</p></p>
