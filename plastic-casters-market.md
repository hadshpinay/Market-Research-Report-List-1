<p><p>The plastic casters market includes various types, each utilizing different materials for specific applications. PU (Polyurethane) casters offer durability and shock absorption, ideal for heavier loads. PA (Polyamide) casters are resistant to wear and temperature, making them suitable for industrial environments. PP (Polypropylene) casters are lightweight and cost-effective, used in lighter equipment. PE (Polyethylene) casters provide excellent chemical resistance. POM (Polyoxymethylene) casters feature low friction and high strength, well-suited for precision equipment, while PC (Polycarbonate) casters are known for their impact resistance and transparency.</p></p>
