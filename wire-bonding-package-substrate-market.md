<p><strong>The Wire Bonding Package Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>RF Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wire bonding package substrate market consists of various types, including WB BGA (Wire Bonding Ball Grid Array) and WB CSP (Wire Bonding Chip Scale Package). WB BGA features a network of solder balls that connect the chip to the substrate, ensuring efficient thermal and electrical performance. WB CSP is smaller and allows for a compact design, integrating the die directly onto the substrate. The RF module segment focuses on high-frequency applications, emphasizing reliability and performance in communication technologies.</p></p>
