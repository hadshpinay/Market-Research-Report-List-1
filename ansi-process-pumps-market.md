<p><strong>The ANSI Process Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sealless</li><li>Sealded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ANSI process pumps market is categorized into sealless and sealed types. Sealless pumps utilize magnetic drive or other technologies to eliminate seals, preventing leaks and enabling safe handling of hazardous fluids. These are ideal for applications requiring minimal maintenance and enhanced reliability. Sealed pumps, on the other hand, employ mechanical seals to contain the process fluid, offering a tried-and-true solution with a wide range of customization options. Each type serves different operational needs based on fluid handling requirements.</p></p>
