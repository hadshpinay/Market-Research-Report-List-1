<p><strong>The Industrial Cartridge Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-in Cartridge Valve</li><li>Slip-in Cartridge Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cartridge valves market is primarily categorized into screw-in cartridge valves and slip-in cartridge valves. Screw-in cartridge valves are designed for easy installation and maintenance, allowing them to be directly threaded into a manifold. They offer versatility and compactness. Slip-in cartridge valves, on the other hand, are inserted into a pre-machined cavity or manifold, providing a secure fit and ease of replacement. Both types serve critical roles in hydraulic systems, optimizing fluid control and enhancing overall efficiency.</p></p>
