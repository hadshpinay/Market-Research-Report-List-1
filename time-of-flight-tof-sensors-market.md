<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are crucial for depth perception in various applications, including robotics and augmented reality. Direct ToF sensors measure the time it takes for light to travel directly to an object and back, providing accurate distance measurements in real-time. Indirect ToF sensors, on the other hand, infer distance based on phase shifts of modulated light waves, offering advantages in lower light conditions and greater range. Both types cater to different needs in imaging and sensing technology.</p></p>
