<p><p>WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) flooring, known for its durability and aesthetic appeal, primarily utilizes three types of plastics: PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), PE (Polyethylene), and PP (Polypropylene). PVC WPC offers waterproof properties and is often used in high-moisture areas. PE WPC is more flexible and resistant to chemicals, making it suitable for various applications. PP WPC, known for its strength and thermal stability, is often preferred for outdoor settings. Each type caters to different consumer needs and environments.</p></p>
