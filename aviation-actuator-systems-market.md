<p><p>The aviation actuator systems market encompasses various types, including electric, electromechanical, electrohydraulic, and electrohydrostatic systems. Electric actuators utilize electric motors to drive movement, providing precise control and quick response times. Electromechanical actuators combine electrical and mechanical components to translate electrical signals into motion. Electrohydraulic systems use hydraulic fluid to transmit force, offering high power and efficiency for larger applications. Electrohydrostatic actuators blend hydraulic power with compact design, enabling high performance in confined spaces, often used in modern aircraft systems.</p></p>
