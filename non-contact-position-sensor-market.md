<p><p>The Non-Contact Position Sensor market comprises two main types: linear and rotary position sensors. Linear position sensors measure the position along a straight line, often utilizing technologies like capacitive or magnetic sensing for accurate measurement without physical contact. Rotary position sensors, on the other hand, gauge the angle or rotation of a shaft, frequently employing technologies such as optical or inductive sensing. Both types are crucial in various applications, including industrial automation, robotics, and automotive systems, ensuring precise position tracking and control.</p></p>
