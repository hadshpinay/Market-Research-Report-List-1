<p><ul><li>Net Type</li><li>Laminated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural irrigation filters market features primarily two types: net filters and laminated filters. Net filters consist of a mesh design that effectively captures larger particles and debris, making them suitable for various irrigation systems. In contrast, laminated filters utilize multiple layers of materials to offer enhanced filtration efficiency and longevity, targeting finer contaminants. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining water quality, preventing clogging in irrigation lines, and ensuring optimal crop health by providing clean, reliable water supply.</p></p>
