<p><p>The Fluorescence Microarray Scanner market is categorized into three main types: Single-color Detection, Two-color Detection, and Others. Single-color Detection utilizes one fluorescent dye to measure gene expression levels for simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Two-color Detection employs paired dyes to compare two samples simultaneously, enhancing sensitivity and allowing for direct comparisons. The "Others" category includes advanced technologies and specialized scanners catering to niche applications, providing flexibility and innovation in fluorescence imaging and analysis in various research fields.</p></p>
