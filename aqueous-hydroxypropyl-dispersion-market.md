<p><ul><li>Primary Dispersion</li><li>Secondary Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aqueous Hydroxypropyl Dispersion market can be divided into two main types: Primary Dispersion and Secondary Dispersion. Primary Dispersion involves the initial mixing of hydroxypropyl components with water, ensuring a uniform distribution of particles. In contrast, Secondary Dispersion refers to further processing where pre-dispersed particles are reintroduced into a new aqueous medium, enhancing stability and performance. Both types are crucial for various applications, including pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food products, offering unique benefits in formulation and functionality.</p></p>
