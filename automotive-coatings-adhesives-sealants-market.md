<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive coatings, adhesives, and sealants market is categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based products utilize organic solvents for application, offering excellent performance in terms of durability and adhesion, but they can emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising environmental concerns. Conversely, water-based products use water as a primary solvent, providing lower VOC emissions and reduced environmental impact, making them increasingly popular for manufacturers seeking eco-friendly solutions. Both types play crucial roles in automotive assembly and finishing processes.</p></p>
