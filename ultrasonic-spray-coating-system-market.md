<p><p>The Ultrasonic Spray Coating System market is primarily divided into two types: Rotary-Type and Flat-Type systems. Rotary-Type Ultrasonic Spray Coating Systems utilize a rotating nozzle to create a fine mist of liquid, ideal for achieving uniform coatings on cylindrical objects. In contrast, Flat-Type Ultrasonic Spray Coating Systems are designed for flat surfaces, ensuring even distribution across larger, flat areas. Each system offers unique advantages tailored to specific applications in various industries, enhancing coating efficiency and quality.</p></p>
