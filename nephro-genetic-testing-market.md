<p><strong>The Nephro Genetic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Next Generation Sequencing</li><li>Multiplex Ligation-dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nephro genetic testing market is primarily categorized into two types: Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) and Multiplex Ligation-dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA). NGS allows for comprehensive analysis of multiple genes simultaneously, enabling precise identification of genetic mutations related to kidney diseases. In contrast, MLPA focuses on detecting copy number variations, making it effective for identifying deletions or duplications in specific genes. Both methods enhance genetic diagnosis and personalized treatment strategies for patients with renal disorders.</p></p>
