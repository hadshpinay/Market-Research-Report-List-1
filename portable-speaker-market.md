<p><p>The portable speaker market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired speakers. They are popular for outdoor and on-the-go use. In contrast, wired speakers connect via cables, often providing superior audio quality and stability. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
