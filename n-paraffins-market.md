<p><p>The n-Paraffins market is divided into two main types: Light Type and Heavy Type. Light Type n-Paraffins typically include hydrocarbons with carbon chains ranging from C5 to C12, and are primarily used as solvents, detergents, and in the production of specialty chemicals. Heavy Type n-Paraffins consist of longer carbon chains, generally from C13 to C30, and are utilized in applications like lubricant base stocks, wax production, and as feedstock in petrochemical processes. These distinctions cater to varying industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
