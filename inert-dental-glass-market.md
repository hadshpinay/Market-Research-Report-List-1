<p><strong>The Inert Dental Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Bioactive Glass"</li><li>"Non-bioactive Glass"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inert dental glass market is categorized into two main types: bioactive glass and non-bioactive glass. Bioactive glass interacts with biological tissues, promoting cell attachment and regeneration, making it ideal for restorative dentistry and implants. In contrast, non-bioactive glass is inert and does not bond with tissues but is used for applications requiring durability and aesthetic properties, such as dental crowns and dentures. Both types cater to specific dental needs, enhancing patient outcomes and material performance.</p></p>
