<p><p>The VR gaming controller market is divided into two primary types: "All-in-one" and "Detached" controllers. All-in-one controllers integrate motion sensors and tracking systems within the headset, offering a seamless experience without external devices. In contrast, detached controllers operate independently from the headset, often utilizing external sensors for tracking, providing precision and an expanded range of motion. Each type caters to different preferences, with all-in-one controllers simplifying setup, while detached options enhance immersion and gameplay flexibility.</p></p>
