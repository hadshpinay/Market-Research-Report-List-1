<p><p>The commercial potato peeler market features primarily two types: commercial abrasive potato peelers and commercial steam potato peelers. Commercial abrasive peelers utilize abrasive surfaces to effectively remove the skin from potatoes, making them suitable for high-volume operations. In contrast, commercial steam peelers use steam to loosen the skin, allowing for a gentler peeling process which helps preserve the texture and quality of the potatoes. Both types cater to the foodservice and processing industries, enhancing efficiency and productivity in potato preparation.</p></p>
