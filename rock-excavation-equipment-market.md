<p><p>The rock excavation equipment market is primarily divided into two types: normal excavation equipment and Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs). Normal excavation equipment includes tools such as excavators, drills, and loaders, utilized for surface and shallow rock removal. In contrast, TBMs are highly specialized machines designed for deep underground tunneling, offering precision and efficiency in hard rock environments. Both categories cater to distinct applications in construction and mining, reflecting varying technological requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
