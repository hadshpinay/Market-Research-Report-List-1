<p><p>The digital pneumatic tourniquet market is categorized into single bladder and dual bladder tourniquets. Single bladder tourniquets utilize one inflatable compartment to apply pressure, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for various surgical procedures. In contrast, dual bladder tourniquets feature two compartments, allowing for more refined pressure control and improved patient safety during complex surgeries, especially in limb surgeries. Both types enhance operational efficiency and can be integrated with digital monitoring systems for precise management of blood flow.</p></p>
