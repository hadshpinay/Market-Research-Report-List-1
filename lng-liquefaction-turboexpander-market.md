<p><p>The LNG liquefaction turboexpander market includes several types, primarily centrifugal and axial-flow turboexpanders. Centrifugal turboexpanders use high-speed rotation to convert pressure energy into mechanical work, making them suitable for large-scale applications with high flow rates. Axial-flow turboexpanders, on the other hand, utilize an axial movement of gas through rotor blades, offering higher efficiency for specific conditions. Additionally, "Others" may encompass hybrid designs and specialized turboexpanders tailored for unique operational requirements or emerging technologies in LNG processing.</p></p>
