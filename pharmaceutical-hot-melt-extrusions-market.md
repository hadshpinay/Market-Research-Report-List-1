<p><p>Pharmaceutical hot melt extrusion (HME) is a processing technique used to enhance drug delivery by formulating solid dosage forms. This method involves the melting of excipients and active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) under controlled temperatures to create a homogenous mixture, which is then extruded into various shapes and sizes. HME offers numerous advantages, including improved bioavailability, controlled release properties, and the ability to incorporate poorly soluble drugs.</p><p>The Pharmaceutical Hot Melt Extrusions Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Hot Melt Extrusions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-Screw Extruder</li><li>Single-Screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical hot melt extrusion market includes two primary types of extruders: twin-screw extruders and single-screw extruders. Twin-screw extruders utilize two intermeshing screws for enhanced mixing and processing efficiency, making them suitable for complex formulations and polymers. In contrast, single-screw extruders utilize a single screw for material conveyance, which is simpler and generally cost-effective, but may have limitations in processing diverse formulations. Both types play crucial roles in developing solid dosage forms and improving drug solubility.</p></p>
