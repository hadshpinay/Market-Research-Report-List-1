<p><p>Atomic-force Microscopy (AFM) is a high-resolution imaging technique that allows scientists to obtain detailed information about the surface properties of materials at the nanoscale. Utilizing a cantilever with a sharp tip, AFM provides topographical maps by scanning the surface and measuring forces between the tip and the sample. This technique is indispensable across various fields such as materials science, biology, and nanotechnology, enabling researchers to explore surface structures, mechanical properties, and molecular interactions.</p><p>The Atomic-force Microscopy (AFM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period.
