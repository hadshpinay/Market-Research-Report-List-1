<p><ul><li>Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press</li><li>Web-Fed Offset Printing Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Offset Lithography Printing Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Sheet-Fed and Web-Fed Offset Printing Presses. Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Presses handle individual sheets of paper, making them ideal for high-quality prints with precise color control, often used for short to medium print runs like brochures and packaging. Web-Fed Offset Printing Presses, on the other hand, feed continuous rolls of paper, allowing for high-speed production ideal for large volumes such as newspapers and magazines. Both types serve distinct printing needs efficiently.</p></p>
