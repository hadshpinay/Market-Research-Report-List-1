<p><p>The AC ammeter market includes two primary types: analog ammeters and digital ammeters. Analog ammeters utilize moving coil mechanisms to display current readings on a dial, offering simplicity and reliability for basic applications. In contrast, digital ammeters provide precise measurements through electronic displays, often featuring advanced functions like data logging and connectivity options. While analog ammeters are valued for their durability and ease of use, digital ammeters are preferred for accuracy and functionality in modern applications.</p></p>
