<p><ul><li>Tag Molecular Beacon</li><li>Label-free Molecular Beacon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Molecular Beacon Probe market is primarily categorized into two types: Tag Molecular Beacons and Label-free Molecular Beacons. Tag Molecular Beacons are designed to incorporate specific labels, allowing for detection through fluorescence when the probe binds to its target nucleic acid sequence. In contrast, Label-free Molecular Beacons utilize inherent properties of the target molecules for detection, eliminating the need for attached labels. Both types play vital roles in applications like diagnostics, genetic research, and real-time PCR, enhancing sensitivity and specificity.</p></p>
