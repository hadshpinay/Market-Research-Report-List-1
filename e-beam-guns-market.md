<p><ul><li>Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns</li><li>Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-Beam Guns market comprises two primary types: Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns and Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns. Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns feature multiple pockets for electron emission, allowing for higher efficiency and improved material deposition in various applications, such as semiconductor manufacturing and surface treatment. In contrast, Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns focus on a single emission point, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications requiring precise deposition but on a smaller scale. Both types cater to distinct industry needs and technological requirements.</p></p>
