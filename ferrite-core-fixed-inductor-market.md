<p><ul><li>Multi-layer Type</li><li>Wire Wound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ferrite Core Fixed Inductor market primarily consists of two types: multi-layer type and wire wound type. Multi-layer inductors are constructed by stacking multiple layers of ferrite material, allowing for compact designs and higher inductance values, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. In contrast, wire wound inductors are created by winding wire around a ferrite core, offering robustness and higher current handling capabilities. Both types cater to diverse applications in electronics, enhancing performance and efficiency in power management and signal processing.</p></p>
