<p><p>The rapid absorbable sutures market is divided into two primary types: natural absorbable sutures and synthetic absorbable sutures. Natural absorbable sutures, typically derived from animal sources, offer inherent biocompatibility and are broken down by enzymatic processes in the body. Synthetic absorbable sutures, made from polymers, are designed to provide consistent absorption rates and mechanical strength. Both types cater to various surgical applications, enhancing healing while minimizing the need for suture removal, thus improving patient comfort and recovery.</p></p>
