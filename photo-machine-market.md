<p><ul><li>Thermal Foaming Photo Machine</li><li>Piezo Photo Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo machine market is categorized into two main types: thermal foaming photo machines and piezo photo machines. Thermal foaming photo machines utilize heat to create images on various substrates, popular for their efficiency and quality in producing vibrant prints. In contrast, piezo photo machines employ piezoelectric technology to control ink droplets, allowing for precise and high-resolution printing. Both types cater to diverse applications in commercial printing, graphic design, and photography, each offering unique benefits to meet specific customer needs.</p></p>
