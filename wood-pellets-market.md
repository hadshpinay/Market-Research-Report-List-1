<p><strong>The Wood Pellets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Pellet</li><li>Black Pellet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood pellets market is primarily categorized into white pellets and black pellets. White pellets, typically made from clean wood residues, are primarily used for heating and electricity generation in residential and industrial applications. They offer high energy density and low ash content. Black pellets, on the other hand, are produced from lignocellulosic biomass and are designed for co-firing in coal power plants or dedicated biomass facilities. They have higher energy content and moisture resistance, making them suitable for transport and storage.</p></p>
