<p><p>The dental anaesthetics market comprises several key types, primarily including lidocaine, mepivacaine, prilocaine, and articaine. Lidocaine is widely used for its rapid onset and effective pain relief. Mepivacaine offers a similar effect but with a longer duration, making it suitable for extended procedures. Prilocaine has low toxicity and is often used in combination with other agents. Articaine features a unique ester and amide structure, providing efficient nerve block and rapid absorption. Other market segments encompass additional less common anaesthetics.</p></p>
